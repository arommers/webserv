Features

Everything you need in a modern webserver and more.

100% non blocking

Experience seamless performance with our web server, powered by poll and I/O multiplexing. Our 100% non-blocking architecture ensures maximum efficiency and responsiveness, handling multiple connections simultaneously without any slowdowns. Say goodbye to bottlenecks and hello to unparalleled speed and reliability. Start serving and see the difference for yourself!

Empower Your Web experience with CGI

Unlock powerful server-side scripting with our web server using Common Gateway Interface (CGI). This versatile technology enables efficient script execution for dynamic content generation, form processing, database interactions, and user authentication. Whether creating personalized experiences, generating real-time data, or managing complex processes, our CGI offers dependable, flexible, and high-performance solutions. Elevate your web experience and harness the full potential of server-side scripting!

Scale Seamlessly

Achieve unparalleled scalability with our advanced web server. Designed to grow with your demands, it handles increasing traffic and data loads effortlessly, ensuring consistent performance and reliability. Whether managing a small site or a large-scale application, our server adapts to your needs, providing the flexibility and power to support your expansion. Experience seamless scaling as your demands evolve.
